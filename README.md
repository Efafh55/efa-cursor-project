AI-Powered SEO Content Research

## Overview
This project focuses on researching modern SEO strategies, especially in the context of AI-powered content production and search optimization.

The goal is to collect high-quality insights from experienced SEO practitioners and build a strong foundation for developing a real-world AI SEO playbook.

---

## Selected Experts

The experts were carefully chosen to represent different aspects of SEO:

- **Execution & SEO Tactics:** Matt Diggity, Nathan Gotch  
- **AI SEO & Automation:** Julian Goldie, Jesse Cunningham  
- **Content & Blogging:** Adam Enfroy  
- **Authority & Systems:** Authority Hacker  
- **Optimization Tools:** Surfer SEO  
- **Strategy & Search Behavior:** Cyrus Shepard  
- **AI Search Evolution:** Nico  
- **Long-form Strategy:** SEO Podcast  

This mix ensures coverage from traditional SEO fundamentals to modern AI-driven search systems.

---

## Data Collection Method

- YouTube transcripts were manually collected using available transcript features
- Content was cleaned and structured for readability
- Due to API limitations, manual collection was used as an alternative approach
- Selected excerpts were used to highlight key insights

## Key Takeaways

- SEO is evolving beyond keywords into intent, structure, and authority
- AI is transforming how content is created, optimized, and discovered
- Scalable systems and workflows are becoming more important than individual tactics
- Future SEO success depends on adapting to AI-driven search engines

## Conclusion

This research serves as a foundation for building an AI-powered SEO playbook by combining insights from multiple industry experts and identifying common patterns across their strategies.
